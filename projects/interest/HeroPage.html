Notre Dame Hero Page

Marcus Freeman

Marcus Freeman started to work at Notre Dame as a defensive back coach in 2021. He worked under the head coach Brian Kelly. He found amazing results in his position and lead to many victories on the defense, due to his coaching. He was able to make Notre Dame one of the top defenses in the country throughout the years.

Success as a coach

Marcus Freeman was named as the head coach for Notre Dame in 2022. Many people thought he didn't have the experience to be a head coach. He has been able to make Notre name nationally ranked every year since he took over. He has been able to take them to play in a National Championship for the first timein decades. It has been outstanding to see the progress he has had with the team.
