Notre Dame Football

I am highly interested in Notre Dame Football. My family lives close by the stadium and I grew up watching them. When I was younger I always dreamed of playing football at the school. In high school, I played all 4 years of football.

National Championship

This year Notre Dame was able to make it to the National Championship game against Ohio State. It was a great game with Notre Dame putting a great effort to win. They came up short and did not win, but they made it really far. It was the first 12-team playoff in NCAA history. They beat teams that included Penn State and Georgia to make it there.
