Welcome to Mongolia

Welcome to Mongolia, a country made of steppes, breathtaking landscapes, and a deeply rooted nomadic culture. This site will guide you to the places you must see, foods, and customs that make Mongolia such a special destination.

Image source: Genghis Khan Equestrian Statue

What You Will Find on This Site:

  • Top destinations such as the Gobi Desert, Lake Khövsgöl, Terelj National Park, and Ulaanbaatar
  • An introduction to Mongolian culture, festivals, and traditions
  • Famous dishes like buuz, khuushuur, and airag
  • A form to help you plan your trip
  • Travel tips for weather, packing, and etiquette

Whether you're looking for adventure, history, or peaceful nature, Mongolia offers an unforgettable journey. Travel through the land of the eternal blue sky and experience the rhythm of nomadic life.

Fun Fact: Mongolia has more horses than people!
