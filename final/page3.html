Traditional Mongolian Cuisine

Mongolian food is simple and hearty. Most meals are based on meat, flour, and dairy. These foods have helped nomads survive in harsh winters for centuries.

This table shows three common traditional foods in Mongolia and when people usually eat them.

Popular Mongolian Dishes
Dish Main Ingredients When It's Eaten Fun Fact
Buuz Meat and flour (steamed dumplings) Lunar New Year Families make hundreds together
Khuushuur Fried dough with meat Festivals and street food It's like a fried version of Buuz
Airag Fermented mare's milk Summer celebrations Offered to guests in countryside
